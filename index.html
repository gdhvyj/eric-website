Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Your answers are below.

What does a financial advisor do?

A financial advisor helps you create strategies for eliminating financial risk and building wealth over the long term. This includes investment management, retirement planning, estate planning, and more, tailored to your specific financial goals.

How often will we review my plan?

Typically, we review plans on a quarterly or semi-annual basis. However, the frequency can be adjusted based on your individual needs, market conditions, or significant life events.

What fees do you charge?

My fee structure is transparent and typically based on a percentage of assets under management. For specific services, a fixed fee may apply. We will discuss all fees upfront before any commitment.

Can you manage my existing investment portfolio?

Yes, I can review your existing portfolio, assess its alignment with your financial goals, and recommend adjustments or manage it on your behalf to optimize performance and manage risk.
